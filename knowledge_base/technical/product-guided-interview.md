# Product: Guided Interview

## What It Does

AI asks 3-5 targeted questions about your product, audience, pain points, and the type of language you're looking for. Builds a structured research brief from your answers that feeds into query generation. Alternative to Quick Mode (single-textarea brief).

Can reference previous sessions with @ to avoid repeating covered ground.

## Key Campaign Messaging

- "Two minutes answering questions can mean the difference between generic threads and laser-targeted conversations."
- "Even if you've never done market research before, the Guided Interview helps you focus."
- "Better briefs produce better results."
