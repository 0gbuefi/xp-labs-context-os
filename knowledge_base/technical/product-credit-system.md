# Product: Credit System & Pricing

## Pricing Tiers

- **Interest** ($29/month): 100 credits/month, ~5 sessions
- **Desire** ($99/month): 400 credits/month, ~20 sessions
- **Action** ($499/month): 2,200 credits/month, ~110 sessions

## Key Details

- 20 credits per research session (fixed, predictable)
- 2-credit buffer charged upfront, refunded on successful completion
- Full refund if session fails
- Follow-up (saturation) rounds: 4 credits each, 60-credit cap per session
- Top-up packs: 25 to 9,000 credits
- Credits valid for 6 months from purchase
- **40 free credits on signup, no credit card required** (~2 full sessions)
- All plans include full feature set

## Key Campaign Messaging

- "40 free credits. No credit card. Two full research sessions to test it."
- "$29/month replaces 10-20 hours of manual research (5 sessions × 2-4 hours each)"
- "Fixed 20-credit pricing per session. No surprises."
