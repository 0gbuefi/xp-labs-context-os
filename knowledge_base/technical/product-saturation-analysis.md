# Product: Saturation Analysis

## What It Does

Scores research coverage across seven dimensions (emotional language, practical objections, identity signals, purchase triggers, etc.). Provides a hero completeness score (0-100), per-dimension scores, gap analysis, and a recommendation: keep researching, research selectively, or move on to writing.

75+ = write confidently. Below 50 = significant gaps.

## Key Campaign Messaging

- "Stop guessing when you have 'enough' research. Saturation analysis tells you with data."
- "Scores your coverage across 7 dimensions and tells you exactly where to go deeper."
- "The data-backed decision point: keep researching or start writing?"
