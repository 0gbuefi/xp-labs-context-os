# Product: 3-Tier Research Pipeline

## What It Does

After launching a research session, XP Labs runs a three-tier pipeline:
- **Tier 1 (Wide):** 15-25 broad queries across multiple categories (pain, desires, triggers, objections, identity, comparisons)
- **Tier 2 (Focused):** 5-10 follow-up queries drilling into the strongest Tier 1 results
- **Tier 3 (Edge Cases):** 3-5 queries surfacing niche, contrarian, or surprising perspectives

Each tier builds on previous results. Total: 25+ queries in ~90 seconds.

## Research Posture Settings

- **Targeted:** Fewer queries, focused on specific angles (Tier 1: 8-14 queries, saves credits)
- **Discovery:** Broad coverage across multiple categories (Tier 1: 15-22 queries)
- **Angle Mining:** Moderate count aimed at finding distinctive copy angles (Tier 1: 10-16 queries)

## Key Campaign Messaging

- "25+ queries across categories you wouldn't have thought of — including edge cases and contrarian perspectives"
- "Three levels of depth in a single session, automatically. 90 seconds."
- "Tier 3 edge cases often produce the most distinctive copy angles"
