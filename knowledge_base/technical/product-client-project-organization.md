# Product: Client/Project Organization

## What It Does

Organize research sessions under clients and projects. Assign sessions during intake. Dashboard groups sessions inside client accordions. Create, rename, and delete clients/projects from the dashboard or intake form. Reassign sessions from the session detail header.

## Key Campaign Messaging

- "Your session count grows fast across multiple clients. Client/project grouping keeps everything navigable."
- "One click to expand a client, one click to find the session."
- "Your supplement client's research never mixes with your fitness client's."

## Why It Matters for Campaigns

Strong selling point for the agency ICP ([[icp-copywriting-agencies]], [[icp-content-marketing-agencies]]) — their biggest organizational headache is keeping research separate across clients.
