# Product: Workspace/Team Collaboration

## What It Does

A workspace is a shared environment for research sessions, credits, and billing. Invite members by email, everyone sees the same sessions, bookmarks, and shares the same credit balance. Solo users can ignore this — XP Labs creates a default workspace automatically.

Workspace switcher in the app header for users on multiple workspaces.

## Key Campaign Messaging

- "When one team member finds gold, the whole team can use it."
- "One subscription, shared credits, shared research — no duplicate accounts."
- "Solo? Ignore workspaces entirely. Team? Share everything automatically."

## Why It Matters for Campaigns

Key differentiator for agency ICP. Solo copywriter messaging doesn't need this. But for [[icp-copywriting-agencies]] and [[icp-content-marketing-agencies]], shared workspace turns XP Labs from an individual tool into a team research platform.
