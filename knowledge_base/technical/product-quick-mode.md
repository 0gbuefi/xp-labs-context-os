# Product: Quick Mode

## What It Does

Skip the Guided Interview and go straight to research. Paste or type your brief into a single text area and click Launch. Same three-tier pipeline, same quality scoring — just a faster brief creation for users who know what they want.

## Key Campaign Messaging

- "When you know your product and audience, Quick Mode shaves minutes off the process."
- "A sentence or a full prompt — your choice. XP Labs handles the rest."
- "Experienced users default to Quick Mode for repeat research."

## Why It Matters for Campaigns

Demonstrates that XP Labs respects the user's expertise. Experienced copywriters don't want to be walked through a questionnaire every time. Two modes = flexible for beginners AND experts.
