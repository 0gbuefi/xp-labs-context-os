# Product: Next Round Research

## What It Does

When saturation shows low scores on specific dimensions, launch a targeted follow-up round. Options:
- **Focus next round here:** Click a low-scoring dimension to immediately target that gap
- **Guide me:** AI conversation to refine the follow-up direction

Runs the same three-tier pipeline but focused on the specific gap. New findings merge into existing themes — no duplicates, no lost work.

## Key Details

- 4 credits per follow-up round
- 60-credit hard cap per session
- Avoids repeating already-covered ground

## Key Campaign Messaging

- "Fill research gaps surgically instead of running another broad session."
- "Saturation shows you're thin on 'purchase triggers.' One click, and XP Labs targets exactly that."
