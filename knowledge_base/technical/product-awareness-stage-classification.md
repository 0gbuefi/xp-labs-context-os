# Product: Awareness Stage Classification

## What It Does

Tags every excerpt with an awareness stage based on Eugene Schwartz's five levels:
- **Unaware:** Doesn't know they have a problem. Great for cold traffic and pattern-interrupt headlines.
- **Problem Aware:** Knows something is wrong but hasn't looked for solutions. Perfect for problem-agitation copy.
- **Solution Aware:** Comparing options. Maps to mid-funnel positioning copy.
- **Product Aware:** Deciding whether to buy. Bottom-funnel sales page language.
- **Most Aware:** Has used the product. Fuels testimonial-style copy and retention messaging.
