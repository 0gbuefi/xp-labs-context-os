# Product: Cross-Session Search

## What It Does

Search across every research session in your workspace at once. Type a keyword or phrase, get matching excerpts from all sessions regardless of when they ran or which client they belong to.

## Key Campaign Messaging

- "Six months from now, search 'price objection' and instantly find every relevant quote from every session you've ever run."
- "Your research history becomes a searchable voice-of-customer library."
- "The feature that makes your research compound instead of expire."
