# Product: Data Export

## What It Does

Three export options:
- **Full session export:** Structured Markdown with original brief, all themes, excerpts, awareness-stage tags, and voice profile
- **Individual excerpt copy:** Click copy icon on any excerpt card
- **Filtered bulk copy:** Copy All exports current filtered view as Markdown (e.g., all "Pain" excerpts at once)

## Key Campaign Messaging

- "Research stuck inside a tool is research you won't use. Zero friction between finding and pasting."
- "Export early, export often. Don't wait until research is 'complete' to start writing."
- "Copy all Pain excerpts in one click. Paste directly into your Google Doc."

## Why It Matters for Campaigns

Critical for the AI prompt engineer persona — they need to export excerpts directly into prompt templates. Also important for any persona whose workflow lives outside XP Labs (Google Docs, Notion, etc.).
