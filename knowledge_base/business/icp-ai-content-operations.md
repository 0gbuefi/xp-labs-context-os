# ICP: AI-Powered Content Operations

## Overview
Teams and individuals using AI writing tools (ChatGPT, Claude, Jasper) to produce content at scale. They need better input data — real customer language — to make AI output sound human instead of generic.

## Company Profile

**Firmographics:**
- Industry/Vertical: Various (SaaS, agencies, ecommerce, media)
- Company size: 1-100 employees
- Revenue range: Varies
- Geographic focus: Global
- Stage: Any stage, AI-forward operations

**Behavioral signals:**
- Heavy use of AI writing tools (ChatGPT, Claude, Jasper, Copy.ai)
- Building prompt libraries and templates
- Producing content at high volume with AI assistance
- Frustrated by generic AI output

## Qualification Criteria

**Must-haves:**
- Actively using AI tools for content creation
- Producing customer-facing copy (not just internal docs)
- Aware that AI output needs better input to improve quality

**Nice-to-haves:**
- Building custom prompt libraries
- Testing different AI tools for content quality
- Already supplementing AI with manual research

**Red flags (disqualifiers):**
- Using AI purely for code or technical docs
- No customer-facing content needs

## Pain Points

- [[pain-point-generic-ai-copy]] - AI output sounds generic because input is generic
- [[pain-point-no-customer-language]] - Prompting AI without real customer language as source material
- [[pain-point-limited-query-angles]] - AI generates plausible but not authentic language

## Personas

- [[persona-ai-prompt-engineer]] - Builds and refines AI prompts for content
- [[persona-content-marketer]] - Uses AI tools as part of content workflow
- [[persona-founder-marketing]] - Founder using AI to handle marketing

## Messaging Considerations

**What resonates:**
- Feed your AI the real words humans use, not more AI-generated phrasing
- XP Labs excerpts export directly into prompt templates
- AI writes better copy when given real customer language as input
- The research step that makes AI tools produce better output

**What doesn't work:**
- Anti-AI messaging (they love AI, they just want better input)
- Traditional copywriting methodology pitch

## Campaign Hooks

- "ChatGPT makes up what customers might say. XP Labs finds what they actually said. Feed real language into your prompts."
- "Your AI content sounds generic because the input is generic. Give it real customer language and watch the output transform."
