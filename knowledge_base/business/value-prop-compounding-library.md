# Value Prop: Compounding Voice-of-Customer Library

Most research is disposable. XP Labs compounds your research investment. Every session adds to a searchable, cross-referenced library organized by theme, awareness stage, and research tag. The more you use it, the more valuable it becomes.

## Key Points

- Cross-session search indexes every excerpt from every session
- Bookmarks curate the best phrases into a personal swipe file
- Client/project organization keeps the library navigable as it grows
- Research from one project often applies to adjacent projects
- Credits stay good for 6 months — no "use it or lose it" pressure

## How This Informs Campaigns

Best in longer-form content where you can paint the picture. Less effective in short subject lines (too abstract):
- "Six months from now, every research session you've ever run is searchable. Need 'price objection' language? Search your library. It's right there."
- Strong retention/upsell angle — value increases with usage
