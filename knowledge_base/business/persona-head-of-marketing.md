# Persona: Head of Marketing / CMO

## Overview
Marketing leader who owns messaging strategy, evaluates tools for their team, and needs consistency in how the brand sounds. They evaluate XP Labs as a team tool for shared customer language research.

## Role Details

**Job title(s):**
- Head of Marketing
- VP of Marketing
- CMO
- Marketing Director

**Department:** Marketing

**Seniority level:** VP-level / Director-level

**Reports to:** CEO / COO

**Team size:** 3-20 marketing team members

## Responsibilities

**Primary responsibilities:**
- Own brand messaging and positioning strategy
- Evaluate and approve marketing tools/stack
- Ensure consistent voice across all marketing channels
- Drive customer acquisition and retention metrics

**Success metrics they're measured on:**
- Pipeline generation and conversion rates
- Customer acquisition cost (CAC)
- Brand consistency scores
- Marketing team efficiency and output quality

## Decision-Making Authority

**Buying power:**
- Budget control: Yes ($29-$499/month is within discretionary budget)
- Approval authority: Can sign for team tools

**Role in buying process:**
- Economic buyer for team tools
- May delegate evaluation to a team member

**Typical objections:**
- "We already use [social listening tool]" → [[already-use-social-listening]]
- "My team can research manually" → [[can-search-reddit-myself]]
- "How does this fit into our existing workflow?" → Data export, workspace collaboration

## Pain Points

- [[pain-point-no-customer-language]] - Team writes copy based on assumptions, not customer words
- [[pain-point-unorganized-research]] - No shared repository of customer language across the team
- [[pain-point-generic-ai-copy]] - Team using AI tools produces homogeneous output

- [[icp-content-marketing-agencies]] - Content agency director

## Email Approach

**Subject lines that work:**
- Team efficiency angle ("Your team's copy all sounds the same")
- ROI framing ("What if research took 90 seconds instead of 3 hours?")

**CTA that converts:**
- Team trial / workspace setup
- Voice profiles as a team messaging reference

**What to avoid:**
- Individual copywriter framing
- Technical feature deep-dives
