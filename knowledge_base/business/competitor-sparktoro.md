# Competitor: SparkToro

Audience research tool showing where your audience hangs out online — which websites they visit, social accounts they follow, podcasts they listen to, hashtags they use. Excellent for understanding audience demographics and media consumption.

## Where It Falls Short

- **Audience demographics, not language:** Tells you where they go, not what they say
- **No verbatim excerpts:** Data about audience, not exact words they use
- **No awareness-stage organization:** Output is about behavior, not buying journey
- **No theme extraction:** Doesn't analyze conversations for language patterns
- **Different use case:** Best for ad targeting and media buying, not copywriting

## XP Labs's Key Advantage

SparkToro tells you WHERE your audience is. XP Labs tells you WHAT they say. Different tools for different jobs — they work well together.

## How This Informs Campaigns

Position as complementary, not competitive. Acknowledge SparkToro's value, then pivot:
- "SparkToro tells you your audience follows @marketer_X and reads blog.com. XP Labs tells you they keep saying 'I'd rather take a pay cut than deal with another scope creep nightmare.' One helps you find them. The other helps you write copy that converts them."
