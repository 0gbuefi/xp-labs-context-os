# Competitor: Social Listening Tools (Brandwatch, Mention, Sprout Social)

Enterprise platforms monitoring social media for brand mentions, tracking sentiment trends, and providing dashboards for PR/marketing teams. Cover multiple platforms, offer real-time alerts, produce analytics reports.

## Where They Fall Short

- **Built for brand monitoring, not copywriting:** Answer "what are people saying about us?" not "what words should I use in my copy?"
- **Sentiment dashboards, not verbatim phrases:** Positive/negative/neutral scores, not exact quotes
- **No awareness-stage organization:** Schwartz stages don't exist in these tools
- **No research tags:** Pain, Dream, Obstacle, Trigger, Identity — irrelevant to brand monitoring
- **No voice profiles:** Don't synthesize language patterns
- **Expensive:** $500-$5,000+/month for features a copywriter won't use

## XP Labs's Key Advantage

Social listening tools help you understand what people are saying. XP Labs helps you write copy using the exact words they said. Different tools for different jobs.

## How This Informs Campaigns

Address when prospect mentions existing social listening investment. Don't compete — differentiate:
- "Brandwatch tells you sentiment is 72% positive. XP Labs tells you people keep saying 'I finally stopped dreading Monday mornings.' One gives you a dashboard. The other gives you copy."
