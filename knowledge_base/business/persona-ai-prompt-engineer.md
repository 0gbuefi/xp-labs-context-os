# Persona: AI Prompt Engineer / AI Content Creator

## Overview
Uses AI writing tools (ChatGPT, Claude, Jasper) to produce content. They build prompt libraries and templates, and need better source material — real customer language — to make AI output sound human rather than generic.

## Role Details

**Job title(s):**
- AI Content Creator
- Prompt Engineer
- AI Copywriter
- Content Operations Manager

**Department:** Marketing / Content / Operations

**Seniority level:** IC to Manager-level

**Reports to:** Head of Marketing or Content Director

## Responsibilities

**Primary responsibilities:**
- Build and refine AI prompt templates for content production
- Produce marketing content at scale using AI tools
- Quality-check AI output for authenticity and brand voice
- Research audience language to feed into prompts

**Success metrics they're measured on:**
- Content output volume and quality
- AI content performance vs. human-written
- Prompt efficiency (output quality per prompt iteration)

## Decision-Making Authority

**Buying power:**
- Budget control: Small tool budget (can expense $29-$99/month)
- Approval authority: Can trial tools, may need approval for team license

**Role in buying process:**
- End user / Champion for AI workflow tools

**Typical objections:**
- "ChatGPT can research my audience" → [[different-from-chatgpt]]
- "AI copy is good enough" → [[ai-copy-good-enough]]

## Pain Points

- [[pain-point-generic-ai-copy]] - AI output sounds generic because input is generic
- [[pain-point-no-customer-language]] - Prompting without real customer language as grounding material
- [[pain-point-limited-query-angles]] - AI generates plausible but not authentic language

## Companies They Work At

- [[icp-ai-content-operations]] - Primary workplace
- [[icp-saas-companies]] - SaaS companies using AI for marketing
- [[icp-content-marketing-agencies]] - Agencies scaling with AI

## Email Approach

**Subject lines that work:**
- AI-specific ("Your AI writes generic copy because the input is generic")
- Complement, don't compete ("Make ChatGPT sound human")

**CTA that converts:**
- "Feed real language into your next prompt. Start free."
- Show before/after: AI prompt with vs. without XP Labs excerpts

**What to avoid:**
- Anti-AI messaging (they love AI)
- Traditional copywriting methodology pitch
