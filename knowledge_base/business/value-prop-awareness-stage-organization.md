# Value Prop: Organized by Awareness Stage

Most research tools give you a pile of data and leave you to sort it. XP Labs organizes every excerpt by awareness stage: Unaware, Problem Aware, Solution Aware, Product Aware, and Most Aware. You get the right language for the right section of your copy.

## Key Points

- Five awareness stages map directly to copy structure (opener, body, close)
- Copywriters save hours of manual sorting and categorization
- Different ads/emails target different stages — pull the right language instantly
- Eugene Schwartz's framework is the standard in direct response copywriting
- No competitor (social listening, Reddit monitoring, SparkToro) offers awareness-stage classification

## Evidence

[VERIFIED: xp-labs-context-os/knowledge_base/value-props/awareness-stage-organization.md] "A long-form sales page is a journey. You open with language for problem-aware readers, transition through solution-aware positioning, and close with product-aware decision language."

## How This Informs Campaigns

Key differentiator for experienced copywriters who already think in awareness stages. Less effective for founders/marketers unfamiliar with the framework:
- Use with: [[icp-freelance-copywriters]], [[persona-freelance-copywriter]]
- Skip for: [[persona-founder-marketing]] (they won't know what awareness stages are)
