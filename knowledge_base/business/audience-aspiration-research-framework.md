# Audience: Wanting a Research Framework

"Framework" and "system" were the most requested things in the survey by a wide margin. The desire cuts across experience levels. Copywriters have consumed plenty of theory ("go to Reddit, look for pain points") but lack the step-by-step execution.

The framework is not the goal — the confidence is. The framework is the mechanism that delivers the confidence.

## Audience Language

- "I need a framework"
- "A system to filter quality info from not-so-important info"
- "Blueprint of questions and platforms"
- "I've taken a lot of other market research training and a lot of it says the same things. It's too theoretical."
- "It means I'm confident that I can write proper copy that is tailor-made to the audience"
- "An edge, a system of sorts"
- "A watch-over-my-shoulder style learning process" — they want practical demonstration, not theory
