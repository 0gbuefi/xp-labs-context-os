# Pain Point: Limited Query Angles

When you search Reddit manually, you only search the angles you think of. You miss entire categories of language: identity signals, contrarian perspectives, edge cases, emotional triggers that wouldn't occur to you as search queries. Your research coverage has blind spots you don't know about.

XP Labs's three-tier pipeline generates 25+ queries across categories you wouldn't have thought of, including edge cases and contrarian perspectives.

## Key Points

- Manual searchers are limited to angles they consciously think of
- Categories like identity language, contrarian views, and emotional triggers get missed
- No way to know what you're missing (unknown unknowns)
- XP Labs's Tier 3 specifically targets edge cases and surprising perspectives
- Saturation analysis shows where coverage gaps remain

## Audience Language

Exact phrases copywriters use when describing this pain:

- "Not finding enough spicy insights or any content at all"
- "The only thing I really hate is when I can't find much"
- "I'll run out of platforms"
- "What to do if I can't find enough"
- "You're running out of places to look and you have nothing"
