# Audience: Freelancer to Business Transition

The central career aspiration follows a progression from early Upwork freelancing through established freelancing to building one's own email list and products. Throssell explicitly models this transition for his audience, having gone from pitching at McDonald's on Upwork to earning money "almost on demand" by writing to his own list.

## Audience Language

- "the work was irregular, the money was good but not life-changing"
- "I can write an email to my list and earn money almost 'on demand'"
- "the difference is ... about five years of insanely hard work"
- "building a business for themselves, with their own customer list to sell to"

## Evidence

[VERIFIED: Email, "8 ways to answer the most dreaded question in copywriting" by Daniel Throssell (Oct 15, 2021)] Career trajectory from underpaid freelancer to list owner.

[VERIFIED: Email, "How I learned copywriting with a rubber ball and a Quarter Pounder" by Daniel Throssell (Mar 23, 2022)] Five years of work between Upwork freelancing and owning a business.
