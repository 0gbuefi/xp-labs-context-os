# Persona: Agency Copywriter / Copy Chief

## Overview
Copywriter or copy lead working at a marketing/copywriting agency. They do research and writing for multiple client accounts, and may influence tool purchasing decisions for the team.

## Role Details

**Job title(s):**
- Senior Copywriter
- Copy Chief
- Lead Copywriter
- Creative Director (Copy)

**Department:** Creative / Copy

**Seniority level:** Senior IC or Manager-level

**Reports to:** Creative Director, Agency Owner, or Head of Marketing

## Responsibilities

**Primary responsibilities:**
- Research target audiences for client projects
- Write conversion-focused copy across channels
- Maintain quality standards across the copy team
- Build and maintain client-specific swipe files

**Success metrics they're measured on:**
- Copy quality and client satisfaction
- Campaign performance metrics (open rates, conversion rates)
- Project turnaround time
- Team output quality (if managing writers)

## Decision-Making Authority

**Buying power:**
- Budget control: Influence only (recommends tools to management)
- Approval authority: Can champion a tool but needs sign-off for team purchase

**Role in buying process:**
- Champion / End user — discovers the tool, makes the case for team adoption

**Typical objections:**
- "We already have a research process" → Show how XP Labs enhances it
- "I need to convince my boss" → Provide ROI framing (time saved × hourly rate)

## Email Approach

**Subject lines that work:**
- Reference multiple-client pain ("Research for 5 clients this week?")
- Team efficiency angle

**CTA that converts:**
- Free trial to test on a current project
- Shared workspace pitch for team adoption

**What to avoid:**
- Solo freelancer messaging
- Pricing discussions (they're not the buyer)
