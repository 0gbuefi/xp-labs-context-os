# Audience: Non-Native Copywriter Challenges

A significant and growing segment — "several thousand" non-native subscribers joined Daniel's list in 2022 alone, from Nigeria, Pakistan, Philippines, Vietnam and more. The brutal truth: writing copy in English as a non-native ranges from "a bit hard" to "quadriplegic swimming backstroke across the Atlantic."

The community is split on whether non-natives can succeed — honest advice gets defensive reactions. Practical paths: write copy in native language, work for companies with copy editors, focus on copywriting skills over English skills.

## Audience Language

- "I make so many mistakes in my output it ain't funny"
- "I don't say you can't succeed as a non-native copywriter. I say it's hard."
- "quadriplegic swimming backstroke across the Atlantic"
- "forming this Global Non-Native Copywriting Alliance against me on social media"
