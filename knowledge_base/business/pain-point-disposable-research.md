# Pain Point: Research Is Disposable

Once you finish a project, the research doc gets buried. Six months later, you start from scratch even if you are writing for the same audience. Every research session is a sunk cost — used once, then lost in a folder somewhere.

XP Labs compounds research. Every session adds to a searchable, cross-referenced library you can draw from for months.

## Key Points

- Manual research is use-once-and-lose
- Same audiences get re-researched from scratch
- No cross-project benefit from past work
- XP Labs's cross-session search makes all past research reusable
- The more you use XP Labs, the more valuable the library becomes
