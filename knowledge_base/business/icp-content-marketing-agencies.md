# ICP: Content Marketing Agencies

## Overview
Agencies producing blog content, SEO content, and thought leadership at scale for clients. They need authentic audience language to make content resonate rather than sound generic.

## Company Profile

**Firmographics:**
- Industry/Vertical: Content Marketing Agency
- Company size: 5-50 employees
- Revenue range: $500K-$10M annually
- Geographic focus: US, UK, Global (English-speaking)
- Stage: Established with multiple active clients

**Behavioral signals:**
- Publishing high volumes of content (blogs, guides, email sequences)
- Researching topics and audience language before writing
- Focused on SEO and thought leadership content
- Using tools like Clearscope, SurferSEO, or Ahrefs for content optimization

## Qualification Criteria

**Must-haves:**
- Producing content for multiple clients
- Content writers on staff
- Investing in content quality (not just volume)

**Nice-to-haves:**
- Already doing audience research via Reddit/forums
- Focused on specific niches
- Producing conversion-focused content (not just SEO filler)

**Red flags (disqualifiers):**
- Pure SEO content mills focused on volume over quality
- Agencies that only repurpose existing content

## Pain Points

- [[pain-point-no-customer-language]] - Content sounds like a marketer, not the reader
- [[pain-point-manual-research-time]] - Topic research across clients is time-intensive
- [[pain-point-unorganized-research]] - Research findings scattered and not reusable

## Personas

- [[persona-content-marketer]] - Content writer/strategist who does the research
- [[persona-head-of-marketing]] - Agency owner or content director

## Messaging Considerations

**What resonates:**
- Write content that uses the same words readers use when they talk about a topic
- Theme extraction shows recurring topics worth writing about
- Cross-session search for pulling past research into new articles

**What doesn't work:**
- Direct response copywriting jargon
- Messaging about sales pages and conversion copy

## Campaign Hooks

- "Your audience discusses [topic] on Reddit using specific words. Your content uses different ones. That gap is why it doesn't resonate."
- "Theme extraction shows what people actually talk about in your client's niche. Write about that."
