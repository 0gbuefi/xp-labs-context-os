# ICP: Freelance Copywriters

## Overview
Independent copywriters who handle their own client work end-to-end — research, writing, and delivery. They need efficient research workflows and a growing swipe file of customer language.

## Company Profile

**Firmographics:**
- Industry/Vertical: Freelance Copywriting / Independent Consultant
- Company size: 1 person (solopreneur)
- Revenue range: $50K-$500K annually
- Geographic focus: Global (English-speaking)
- Stage: Established freelancer with active client pipeline

**Behavioral signals:**
- Handling 3+ client projects per month
- Charging $2K+ per project (values research-backed work)
- Already doing some form of audience/Reddit research
- Building personal swipe files and reference libraries
- Active in copywriting communities (Twitter/X, Slack groups, subreddits)

## Qualification Criteria

**Must-haves:**
- Actively writing copy for clients (not just content)
- Does voice-of-customer research as part of process
- Produces direct response, sales pages, or email copy

**Nice-to-haves:**
- Specializes in a niche (health, SaaS, ecommerce, finance)
- Uses Schwartz awareness stages or similar frameworks
- Already familiar with Reddit as a research source

**Red flags (disqualifiers):**
- Hobbyist writers not charging for work
- Academic/technical writers (no VOC need)
- Writers who rely solely on AI generation with no research step

## Pain Points

- [[pain-point-manual-research-time]] - 2-4 hours per project doing manual Reddit research
- [[pain-point-disposable-research]] - Past research gets lost between projects
- [[pain-point-limited-query-angles]] - Only searching the angles they think of

## Personas

- [[persona-freelance-copywriter]] - The buyer IS the user

## Messaging Considerations

**What resonates:**
- Time savings per project (2-4 hours → 90 seconds)
- Building a compounding swipe file over time
- Exact phrases, not AI summaries
- Voice profiles as a writing cheat sheet
- Awareness-stage organization mapping to copy structure

**What doesn't work:**
- Team/enterprise messaging
- Complex onboarding — needs to feel simple and fast

## Campaign Hooks

- "You already search Reddit for customer language. XP Labs does 25+ searches in 90 seconds and organizes everything by awareness stage."
- "What if every research session added to a searchable swipe file you could draw from for months?"
- "Stop copy-pasting Reddit quotes into Google Docs. Start with organized, tagged, copy-ready excerpts."
