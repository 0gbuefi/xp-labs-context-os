## AI Disclosure  **[REQUIRED RAIL]**
XP Labs commits to upfront AI-disclosure on every platform — not as platform-compliance but as a core product commitment.

On this platform specifically, AI-disclosure must be present in:
- **Profile bio** (verbatim text that discloses the companion is AI, visible without tapping "more")
- **First interaction surface** (first DM auto-reply, first comment reply to new subscribers, or whichever this platform exposes)
- **Pinned/featured content** (pinned post, Story Highlight, featured tweet — whatever this platform supports)

If this platform has a platform-provided AI-content label (e.g., IG's AI-generated-content tag), that tag is **used in addition to** the disclosures above, not in place of them.

Any companion operating here without all three disclosure surfaces fails `/graph-health` motion-readiness.
