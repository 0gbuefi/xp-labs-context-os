### Example 2: [Context]
```
Subject: 8-week exit for pottery businesses?

Hi {{FirstName}},

Most pottery and ceramics business owners looking to sell spend 6 months finding the right buyer.

We buy pottery businesses and even closed a similar business in 8 weeks last year.

You'd have two options: we introduce you to buyers in our network, or we buy {{Company}} directly.

If you're looking to sell, worth a quick call to see which fits?
```

**Why this works:**
- "We buy pottery businesses" = immediate clarity
- "looking to sell" in opening = sets context
- "if you're looking to sell" in CTA = qualifier/self-selection
