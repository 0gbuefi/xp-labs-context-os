# Clickbank Top VSLs Competitor Research

## What It Is

Clickbank is a marketplace for digital products (courses, ebooks, supplements, software) where affiliates earn commissions by promoting vendor offers. Its public "gravity" score represents how many affiliates are successfully converting an offer — making it a proxy for copy performance.

**The research insight:** The top-ranked offers by gravity in any B2C niche have the most tested, highest-converting copy. Their sales pages and VSLs (video sales letters) represent validated audience language, proven pain points, and confirmed desires for that market.

---

## Two Access Methods

### Method 1: Clickbank Affiliate Marketplace (Free Account)
1. Go to `accounts.clickbank.com` and sign up for a free affiliate account
2. Navigate to the "Affiliate Marketplace"
3. Browse or select a category from the left sidebar
4. Sort offers by **Gravity** (highest gravity = most converting)
5. Click through to the sales page / VSL of the top offers

### Method 2: CB Engine (No Account Required)
1. Go to `cbengine.com/clickbank-top-gravity.html`
2. Use the filters on the right to select your niche
3. Click the Gravity column header to sort descending
4. Click through to top offers' sales pages

CB Engine is faster for a quick scan — no login required.

---

## What to Look for in Top VSLs

Once you've found a high-gravity offer in your niche, study its sales page or VSL for:

- **The hook / opening problem statement** — How do they name the pain? What situation do they open with?
- **Identity language** — Who does this speak to? How do they define the target customer?
- **The mechanism / villain** — What do they blame for the problem? (Often very copy-rich)
- **Outcome language** — What transformation/result do they promise?
- **Objection handling** — What doubts do they address and how?
- **Urgency language** — How do they frame scarcity or time pressure?

These are the angles that the market has already voted on with their wallets. The language in a top-gravity VSL is more validated than anything you'd find on Reddit — it's been A/B tested against real buyers.

---

## Important Scope Limitation: B2C/Info Products Only

Clickbank is almost exclusively **B2C and info products** — health supplements, fitness programs, financial courses, relationship products, etc.

For **B2B cold email research**, this tool is less directly applicable but can still be useful:
- If the client operates in a market with a consumer crossover (e.g., a B2B product targeting small business owners who also consume B2C business content)
- If you want to understand the broader emotional landscape of a market before going B2B-specific
- For studying VSL structure and copy techniques that can be adapted to cold email

## Gravity Score Interpretation

| Gravity | Interpretation |
|---|---|
| 100+ | Top offer in category, very actively converting |
| 20–100 | Solid performer, worth studying |
| 5–20 | Moderate, may have niche audience |
| <5 | Low-converting or very new — skip |

High gravity = many affiliates making money promoting it = many buyers converting = proven copy.
