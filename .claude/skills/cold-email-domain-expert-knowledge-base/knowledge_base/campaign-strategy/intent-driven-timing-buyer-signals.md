# Intent-Driven Timing: Buyer Signals

Blend campaign data with buyer signals such as **hiring patterns, funding events, product launches, and website visits** to reach prospects when they actually care, not at arbitrary intervals. Right-time outreach converts dramatically higher than calendar-based sequences.

## Core Principles

- **Timing = as important as messaging** - right moment multiplies impact
- **Buyer signals** reveal readiness: hiring, funding, launches, site visits
- **Intent-driven beats calendar-driven** - trigger on events, not dates
- **Combine multiple signals** - hiring + funding = strong intent
- **Window of opportunity** - strike while change is fresh (30-60 days)
- **Relevance compounds** - signal-based personalization feels prescient
- **2026 frontier:** Intelligence-led teams win by timing precision

## When to Use

Use intent-driven timing when:
- Targeting mid-market to enterprise accounts (trackable signals)
- Have access to intent data platforms
- Campaign tied to specific triggers (hiring, expansion, funding)
- Want maximum relevance and conversion
- Can automate signal monitoring
- ROI justifies intent data investment

**Use calendar-based when:**
- SMB market (signals less reliable)
- Mass market approach
- No access to intent tools
- Testing early campaigns (need baseline first)
- Budget constrained

## How to Apply

### Key Buyer Signals

**1. Hiring Patterns**

**Signal:** New job postings, role expansions, team growth

**Why it matters:** Hiring = budget allocated, priorities clear, pain active

**Example triggers:**
- Posted 3+ SDR roles in last 30 days → outbound scaling pain
- Hired first VP Sales → executive onboarding challenge
- Expanded RevOps team → systems/process improvement need
- Added Customer Success roles → retention/expansion focus

**Outreach window:** 7-30 days after posting/hire

**Message angle:**
```
Hey [Name],

Saw you're hiring 5 SDRs this quarter - congrats on the growth!

Most teams struggle to ramp new SDRs in under 90 days when
scaling fast. We built a playbook that gets reps to quota in 45 days.

Used by 12 Series B companies scaling through hypergrowth.

Worth 15 minutes next week?
```

**2. Funding Events**

**Signal:** Announced seed, Series A/B/C/D rounds

**Why it matters:** Capital = buying power, expansion plans, urgency to deploy

**Example triggers:**
- Raised Series A ($5-15M) → building go-to-market foundation
- Raised Series B ($20-50M) → scaling what works
- Raised Series C ($50M+) → market expansion, new geos/verticals
- Raised growth round → specific initiative funding

**Outreach window:** 30-90 days after announcement (post-hiring phase)

**Message angle:**
```
Hey [Name],

Congrats on the Series B! Saw the announcement last month.

Most Series B companies we talk to struggle with the same thing:
scaling go-to-market 3-5x while maintaining efficiency.

We've helped 8 Series B SaaS companies scale from $5M to $20M ARR
without tanking CAC payback. All 8 stayed under 12-month payback.

Quick call make sense to walk through the playbook?
```

**3. Product Launches**

**Signal:** New product announcements, feature releases, market expansions

**Why it matters:** Launch = GTM needs, positioning challenges, demand gen required

**Example triggers:**
- Launched new product line → need separate GTM motion
- Entered new vertical → vertical-specific messaging needed
- Released enterprise tier → upmarket sales playbook required
- Announced new geo → regional go-to-market strategy

**Outreach window:** 14-45 days after launch

**Message angle:**
```
Hey [Name],

Saw you launched the Enterprise tier last month. Big move!

Most companies underestimate the difference between SMB and
Enterprise sales. We've seen 60% fail to hit Enterprise targets year one.

Built an upmarket playbook used by 6 companies successfully moving upmarket.
All 6 hit enterprise quota within 6 months.

15 minutes to share what worked?
```

**4. Website Visit Intent**

**Signal:** Prospect visited your site, specific pages, multiple sessions

**Why it matters:** Active research = problem active in their mind

**Example triggers:**
- Visited pricing page 3+ times → budget evaluation
- Viewed case studies in their industry → solution exploration
- Downloaded content asset → problem education phase
- Attended webinar → actively learning

**Outreach window:** 24-72 hours after visit (strike while hot)

**Message angle:**
```
Hey [Name],

Noticed you checked out our pricing page yesterday.

Happy to answer any questions about how the platform works or walk
through a quick demo tailored to [Company]'s use case.

Does Thursday at 2pm work for 15 minutes?
```

**5. Organizational Changes**

**Signal:** Executive hires, department restructures, office openings

**Why it matters:** Change = new priorities, budget reallocation, fresh perspective

**Example triggers:**
- New CMO hired → marketing strategy refresh
- New CRO hired → sales process overhaul
- Merged departments → systems consolidation
- Opened new office → infrastructure needs

**Outreach window:** 30-60 days after change (settled but still in change mode)

### Multi-Signal Stacking

**Higher intent = multiple concurrent signals:**

**Example: Maximum Intent Stack**
```
Company: Acme Corp
Signals (last 60 days):
✅ Raised Series B ($30M)
✅ Hired VP Sales (30 days ago)
✅ Posted 8 SDR roles
✅ Visited pricing page 4 times
✅ Attended competitor comparison webinar

Intent score: 95/100 (reach out NOW)
```

**Message incorporating stacked signals:**
```
Hey Sarah,

Congrats on the Series B and bringing John on as VP Sales!

Saw you're hiring 8 SDRs to scale outbound. Most Series B companies
we work with struggle to ramp that many reps without sacrificing quality.

Since you attended our SDR scaling webinar last week, figured you're
evaluating options.

We've helped 6 Series B companies (post-funding, similar stage) onboard
8-15 SDRs in 45 days with 85%+ quota attainment.

Worth 15 minutes this week to walk through the playbook?
```

**Why this works:**
- Acknowledges funding (congratulate success)
- References executive hire (shows deep research)
- Connects to hiring pain (timely relevance)
- Notes webinar attendance (shows interest)
- Matches stage and scale (precise relevance)

### Tools for Signal Monitoring

**Intent Data Platforms:**
- **6sense:** Account-level intent, buying stage identification
- **Bombora:** Content consumption signals across web
- **G2:** Product research and comparison signals
- **TechTarget:** IT buyer research activity

**Hiring Signals:**
- **LinkedIn Recruiter:** Job posting alerts
- **Wellfound (AngelList):** Startup hiring trends
- **Greenhouse/Lever APIs:** Job board monitoring

**Funding Data:**
- **Crunchbase:** Funding announcements, valuations
- **PitchBook:** Private company data
- **TC (TechCrunch):** Startup funding news

**Website Intent:**
- **Clearbit Reveal:** Website visitor identification
- **6sense:** Account-based web tracking
- **Koala:** Product-qualified account signals
- **RB2B:** LinkedIn visitor identification

**Org Changes:**
- **LinkedIn Sales Navigator:** Job change alerts
- **People Data Labs:** Employment history API
- **Apollo.io:** Contact + job change tracking

### Automation Setup

**Trigger-based sequences:**

```
IF:
  - Company raises Series A/B/C
  - AND hired VP/CXO in last 30 days
  - AND posted 3+ related job openings

THEN:
  - Wait 21 days (let them settle)
  - Send personalized sequence
  - Reference funding + hire + growth
  - Offer stage-appropriate solution

ELSE:
  - Add to nurture (not ready yet)
```

**Real-time intent triggers:**

```
IF:
  - Prospect visits pricing page
  - AND views case studies
  - AND checks integrations page
  - All within 48 hours

THEN:
  - Send within 24 hours
  - Reference pages viewed
  - Offer demo/call
  - High urgency tone
```
