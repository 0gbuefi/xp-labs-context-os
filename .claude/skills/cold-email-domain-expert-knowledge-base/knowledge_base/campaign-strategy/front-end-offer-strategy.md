# Front-End Offer Strategy

Create a **free lead magnet** (valuable giveaway) or **front-end offer** (low-cost entry product) that reduces friction for cold prospects while creating long-term revenue opportunity.

## Core Principles

- Lead magnet = free, but prospects know it costs YOU money/time
- Front-end offer = cheap entry price, profitable on back-end
- Not a webinar or white paper (too common, low perceived value)
- Must have straight line to cash (not dead-end giveaway)
- Can liquidate customer acquisition cost
- Often creates NEW market (not just switching customers)

## When to Use

Use front-end offers when:
- Main offer isn't getting responses
- Prospects hesitant due to risk/commitment
- Need to demonstrate value before asking for sale
- Can profitably deliver partial service
- Have back-end revenue model

## How to Apply

### LEAD MAGNET (Free but Valuable)

**Criteria:**
✓ Prospects know it costs you money/time
✓ Solves immediate tactical problem
✓ Leads naturally to main offer
✓ Can be delivered at scale

**Good Examples:**
- "Free list of newly hired people in your ICP" (GEX)
- "Free campaign launch with leads sent to your inbox" (GEX)
- "Free deliverability audit" (attempted, didn't work well)
- "Free monthly security training for new employees" (cyber security)

**Bad Examples:**
- ✗ Webinar (everyone does this)
- ✗ White paper (low perceived value)
- ✗ Generic ebook

**GEX Lead Magnet Evolution:**

**Attempt 1: Free Deliverability Audit**
```
Subject: emails in spam?

Hey {{first_name}} – saw you had SDRs like {{name}} on team.
Ran report - your domain doesn't have DMARC.

We send 1.5M emails/month for customers.

Want free deliverability audit?
```
**Result:** Failed. People didn't care enough about the problem.

**Attempt 2: Free Lists**
```
Subject: list?

Hey {{first_name}} – saw you have SDRs on team.

Most popular list = newly hired people in target market.
Looks like you target {{job titles}}.

Want me to make this list and send it to you?
```
**Result:** Better responses, hard to convert to meetings.

**Attempt 3: Free Campaign Launch** ✅
```
Subject: more customers like {{case study}}?

Saw that work you did with {{case study}}.

We help companies get leads with emails like this.
Everything starts with a FREE campaign.

We launch for you, give leads straight to your inbox.
Did this for 7 companies last week.

Want to meet about what a test could look like?

P.S. How you helped {{case study detail}} showcases your value props well.
```
**Result:** Much higher responses. Irresistible offer - actual work done for free.

### FRONT-END OFFER (Paid Entry Product)

**Criteria:**
✓ Liquidates customer acquisition cost
✓ Creates demand for main product
✓ Profitable on back-end
✓ Solves real problem upfront

**Example 1: Listkit (Data Company)**

**Front-end:** $2,500 done-for-you setup
- Email script
- Inboxes configured
- First list provided

**Back-end:** Monthly data subscription
- Customers need ongoing data for system
- Already set up and sending
- Natural upsell, not switching friction

**Why it works:**
1. $2,500 liquidates CAC
2. Creates new customers (not switching from competitor)
3. Sets up dependency on their data platform
4. Upfront cash + recurring revenue

**Example 2: Payment Processor**

**Front-end:** Free AI-built website for local businesses

**Back-end:** Payment processing on all transactions
- Website has their payment processor built in
- Merchant needs processor anyway
- Already integrated, easy path

**Example 3: GEX Free Campaign**

**Front-end:** Completely free campaign launch
- GEX pays for inboxes
- GEX pays for data
- GEX writes script
- Prospect gets actual leads

**Back-end:** Paid scaling if campaign works
- Proven the system works
- Prospect sees ROI before paying
- Natural upsell to ongoing service

**Why it works:**
1. Proves systems work (both parties see results)
2. Zero risk for prospect
3. Clarifies what success looks like
4. Creates switching cost once working

### INFORMATION ARBITRAGE (Insight as Lead Magnet)

When you can't deliver free service, share knowledge prospect doesn't have.

**Pattern:**
- You know something about their market they don't
- Package as insight, audit, or analysis
- Low cost to you, high value to them

**Example:**
"I analyzed your top 10 competitors' Google Ads strategies - noticed 3 gaps you could exploit. Want the report?"
