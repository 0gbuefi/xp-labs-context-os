# Social Trigger: LinkedIn Engagement (2024 Trend)

The **social trigger** was 2024's most effective trigger - targeting people who posted about or engaged with specific topics on LinkedIn. Outperformed traditional triggers like new in role, hiring, and fundraising.

## Core Principles

- LinkedIn engagement = intent signal (they care about topic)
- Works even when pitch unrelated to post (shows you saw their activity)
- Post data (Clay) + engagement data (Trigify) = complete picture
- Each year a new trigger dominates, then becomes saturated
- Get in early on emerging triggers before everyone copies

## When to Use

Use social triggers when:
- Want highest-intent prospects
- Topic-relevant LinkedIn activity exists
- Can access LinkedIn post/engagement data
- Need stronger opener than generic outreach
- Following 2024+ best practices

## How to Apply

**Data Sources:**

**Clay:** Finds LinkedIn posts by prospect
**Trigify:** Finds people who engaged with specific posts/topics

**Two Approaches:**

### APPROACH 1: Target Post Authors

Find people posting about relevant topics:

**Example (Sales Automation Product):**
```
Find people who posted about:
- "outbound challenges"
- "SDR productivity"
- "sales automation"
- "cold email strategy"

They just publicly declared they care about this topic.
```

**Email Template:**
```
Subject: your recent post

Hey {{first_name}} – saw your post about {{AI: summarize post topic}}.

{{Pitch your relevant solution}}

{{Case study}}

Interested in discussing?
```

### APPROACH 2: Target Post Engagers

Find people who liked/commented on relevant posts:

**Example:**
Influencer posts about "sales automation tools" - scrape everyone who engaged.

They're interested in topic (proven by engagement).

**Email Template:**
```
Subject: question

Hey {{first_name}} – noticed you engaged with {{influencer}}'s post about {{topic}}.

{{Pitch your relevant solution}}

Interested?
```

**The Power of Unrelated Pitches:**

Surprisingly, even pitching something unrelated works better with social trigger:

**Example:**
```
"Saw you engaged with post about sales automation.

Btw, we help companies like yours with [completely different service].

Want to chat?"
```

**Why it works:** You demonstrated you know they exist and pay attention to them. Personalization signal, even if pitch isn't directly related.

**Historical Trigger Evolution:**

GEX observed trigger trends:

**2022:** Fundraising trigger dominated
"Saw you raised $X..." → High response rates

**2023:** New in role trigger dominated
"Congrats on new VP Sales role..." → High response rates

**2024:** Social trigger dominated
"Saw your post about..." → Highest response rates

**Pattern:** Each trigger saturates as everyone copies it. Early adopters win.

**Data Collection:**

**Step 1 - Clay:** Find LinkedIn posts
- Scrape LinkedIn profiles
- Extract recent posts
- Filter by keyword relevance

**Step 2 - Trigify:** Find engagement
- Input target post URLs
- Scrape likers and commenters
- Export engaged users

**Step 3 - Combine:**
- Enrich with emails (waterfall)
- Verify emails
- Send to campaign

**Trigger Combinations:**

Social trigger + other signals = ultra-high intent:

**Example:**
```
LinkedIn engagement about sales automation
+ New VP of Sales role
+ Company hiring SDRs
+ Recent funding round

= Perfect storm prospect
```
