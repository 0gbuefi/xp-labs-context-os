# Four-Sentence Email Framework

The generally accepted cold email template structure: **Four sentences under 100 words** (75 words is even better). This framework forces clarity and respects prospect time while delivering complete value proposition.

## Core Principles

- Keep total email under 100 words (target 75)
- Each sentence has a specific job
- Avoid vague corporate language (synergy, leverage, ROI)
- Make every word earn its place
- Structure works across industries and offers

## When to Use

Use this framework when:
- Writing initial cold email templates
- Simplifying overly complex messaging
- Testing new value propositions
- Training team on email fundamentals
- Need proven structure for A/B tests

## How to Apply

**The Four Sentences:**

**Sentence 1: Why You, Why Now**
Explain why you're reaching out to THIS person at THIS time. Either AI-generated or "list becomes message" (targeting so specific the first line is self-evident).

Example: "LinkedIn told me you took over the VP of Sales role at {{company_name}}. I did some digging and saw you don't have an SDR team yet."

**Sentence 2: Poke the Bear OR Explain Offer**
Either agitate pain points with a question about their current approach, OR jump directly into how you help.

Example (poke): "Do you have a plan to build pipeline with such a lean team?"
Example (offer): "We help companies build pipeline with cold email for half the cost of an SDR."

**Sentence 3: Social Proof**
Share case study showing you've done this before with relevant results.

Example: "In the past year, we've helped companies like Prospeo build over $3M in pipeline selling a $99 product with our outbound service."

**Sentence 4: Call to Action**
Ask if they're interested and want to take next step. Keep friction low.

Example: "If we could help you generate pipeline for 2025, would it be useful to connect?"

**Optional: P.S. Line**
Additional value, objection handling, or extra personalization.

Example: "P.S. That free test is really free and we can get it launched in less than 15 minutes on a call together."

**Complete Example (GEX Make More Money Template):**
```
Subject: cheers to the role

Hey {{first_name}} - LinkedIn told me you took over the VP of Sales role at {{company_name}}. I did some digging and saw you don't have an SDR team yet.

Do you have a plan to build pipeline with such a lean team?

In the past year, we've helped companies like Prospeo build over $3M in pipeline selling a $99 product with our outbound service that's half the cost of an SDR.

If we could help you generate pipeline for 2025, would it be useful to connect?

%signature%

P.S. We always start with a free test to validate your offer works with cold email.
```
**Word count: 94 words**
