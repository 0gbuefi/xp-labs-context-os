# AI-Generated Personalization Wins

GEX ran 9 campaign experiments for one client testing various targeting and personalization approaches. **AI-generated personalization campaign outperformed all others** - including new hire triggers, technographics, case study matching, and founder-led sales angles.

## Core Principles

- Use AI for PIECES of copywriting (custom variables), not full email
- AI excels at: relevant first lines, specific value prop applications, custom ideas
- Train AI on: prospect company, your service, value proposition being tested
- People claiming "I can spot AI emails" are wrong - data proves otherwise
- AI + human framework = best results

## When to Use

Use AI personalization when:
- Need to scale personalization beyond manual capacity
- Have clear framework but need custom applications
- Want to test which angles resonate (AI can brainstorm)
- Winning responses reveal patterns (train AI to replicate)
- Combining structured template with dynamic content

## How to Apply

**What AI Should Write:**

✅ **First line (why you, why now):**
"I was on the site and saw how you help [AI-generated mission]..."

✅ **Specific value prop application:**
"Specifically, I think it could help you [AI-generated use case based on their role and company]"

✅ **Custom ideas/suggestions:**
```
I had some ideas for you about [topic]:
- [AI idea 1]
- [AI idea 2]
- [AI idea 3]
```

**What AI Should NOT Write:**

✗ Full email (loses structure and CTA clarity)
✗ Subject line (simple works better)
✗ Social proof (use real case studies)
✗ Core offer description (be precise)

**GEX Campaign Comparison (9 Experiments):**

Client: Sales automation platform

**Lost to AI-generated:**
1. ❌ New hire targeting
2. ❌ Competitor technographic (using competing platform)
3. ❌ Founder-led sales automation angle
4. ❌ Case study matching
5. ❌ 2-sentence straight-to-point
6. ❌ Technographic integration matching
7. ❌ Free community invite
8. ❌ (2 more variations)

**Winner:**
9. ✅ **AI-Generated Campaign**

Significantly higher response rate than all other approaches.

**Why It Won:**

- **Relevance:** AI analyzed prospect's company and role to create specific applications
- **Variety:** Each email slightly different (avoids spam filters, feels personal)
- **Brainstorming:** AI found angles humans didn't think to test
- **Scale:** Can't manually write custom ideas for 1,000 prospects

**AI Training Process:**

**Input to AI:**
1. What prospect's company does (scraped from website)
2. What our service does (product description)
3. Value proposition being tested (save time, make money, etc.)
4. Prospect's role and responsibilities

**Output from AI:**
- Custom first line about their company
- Specific way our service applies to their situation
- OR 3 custom ideas we could help with

**Example (Standard Sequence with AI):**

```
Subject: question

Hey {{first_name}} – {{AI: why we're reaching out based on their company}}

{{Human: Our core value prop}}

Specifically, I think it could help you {{AI: specific application to their role and company}}

{{Human: Case study and CTA}}
```

**Example (Creative Ideas Campaign):**

```
Hey {{first_name}} – I was on the site and saw how you help {{AI: mission statement summary}} and I had some ideas about how we can help with {{topic}}.

{{AI: Custom idea 1}}
{{AI: Custom idea 2}}
{{AI: Custom idea 3}}

The reason I ask is because we help companies {{Human: offer}}.

Would it be useful to discuss executing any of these ideas?
```

**Reviewing AI Output:**

When positive responses come in:
1. Check what AI wrote for that prospect
2. Identify pattern in winning AI responses
3. Train AI to replicate that pattern
4. "Keep copying the winning format"
