# Include Price in Cold Email

When running cold offers (especially pilots or finite engagements), include pricing directly in the email. This creates "holistic clarity" - prospects understand the full picture and can make immediate yes/no decisions. Price transparency differentiates you from competitors hiding costs.

**Core insight:** "That makes me stand out versus you. Because with you... it's well, you're offering to get 10 calls a month... But I'm saying I'm going to get you five calls in the next 30 days. It's only 1,500 which is 300 a call but you'll have to pay 750 up front. If it don't work you don't have to pay anything else. It's like you are going to go with me or at least speak to me because you've got holistic clarity."

## Core Principles

**Why include price:**
1. **Holistic clarity** - Prospect sees full picture (outcome + timeframe + cost + structure)
2. **Immediate decision** - Can say yes or no without needing call to learn price
3. **Differentiation** - Competitors hide price = you stand out by being transparent
4. **Pre-qualification** - Filters out price-sensitive prospects before call
5. **Trust signal** - Transparency indicates confidence in value
6. **You stand out** - While competitors are vague, you provide complete information

**When to include price:**
- Frontend offers (pilots, sprints, finite engagements)
- Offers with clear outcomes and timeframes
- When price is reasonable relative to value
- When you want to filter prospects by budget
- Lead gen 30-day pilots specifically

**When to potentially exclude price:**
- Backend offers (retainers, ongoing services) - these are sold on calls
- High-ticket offers requiring explanation ($50k+)
- Custom/variable pricing based on scope
- When price without context seems high

**Critical:** If you're using a cold offer (finite, specific, safe), price inclusion amplifies those qualities.

## When to Use

Include price in email when:
- Running 30-day pilot or similar sprint offers
- Offer is under $5k (doesn't require explanation)
- Payment structure is simple (flat fee or split payment)
- You have guarantee or risk reversal
- Competition is hiding pricing
- You want faster qualification

**Especially effective for:**
- Lead gen 30-day pilots ($1,500)
- Quick win services ($500-$3,000)
- Trial/POC offers
- Services with clear ROI

**Red flags not to include:**
- You're uncomfortable defending the price
- Price seems high without context/proof
- Deal structure is complex
- Customization required before pricing

## How to Apply

### Pattern 1: Pilot with Split Payment

**Structure:**
```
Subject: 5 calls in 30 days?

[Personalization]

We'll get you 5 qualified [ICP] calls in the next 30 days.

$1,500 total. $750 upfront, $750 when we deliver.

[Social proof if available]

Want details?
```

**Why it works:**
- Specific outcome (5 calls)
- Finite timeframe (30 days)
- Clear price ($1,500)
- Clear structure (split payment)
- Risk reduced (pay half at end)
- Can say yes/no immediately

**Comparison to competitor:**
Competitor: "We'll optimize your pipeline with qualified leads"
- No outcome specificity
- No timeframe
- No price
- Requires call to learn anything

Prospect thinking: "First email is specific, priced, clear. Second is vague. I'll talk to first one."

### Pattern 2: Quick Win with Guarantee

**Structure:**
```
Subject: Reduce payment fees 50%?

We'll reduce your payment processing fees 50% in 24 hours or you don't pay.

$2,000 flat fee (only if we deliver).

Worked for [Similar Company]. Want to see how?
```

**Why it works:**
- Dramatic outcome (50% reduction)
- Ultra-fast (24 hours)
- Risk-free (only pay if delivered)
- Price is clear ($2,000)
- Proof provided (similar company)

### Pattern 3: Free Lead Magnet with Backend Price Signal

**Structure:**
```
Subject: Free GTM strategy for [Company]?

We've built a complete go-to-market strategy + mockup landing page for you.

Free to review. Worth $5k+.

If you want us to implement it, we can talk. Otherwise, it's yours.

Want to see it?
```

**Why mentioning value works:**
- Not charging, but signaling quality ("worth $5k+")
- No obligation
- Backend price anchored (implementation will be in $5k+ range)

### Pattern 4: Pricing Psychology

**Split payment psychology:**
- $1,500 total = $750 + $750
- Feels smaller than $1,500 lump sum
- Backend payment = accountability (we have to deliver)
- "Only pay half now" = reduced risk

**Per-unit pricing:**
- "$1,500 for 5 calls = $300/call"
- Makes price concrete and comparable
- Shows value per outcome

**Risk reversal pricing:**
- "Only pay if we deliver"
- "Half now, half when completed"
- "Or you don't pay"

All reduce perceived risk, making price feel safer
